Realtor Empress

The website that I designed mirrors the light feeling of the branding, incorporating the soothing palette of sage blue, light brown, cream, and a touch of light yellow. I chose these colors for relaxing and inviting ambiance. My goal is to immediately put the users at ease as they navigate the site. By integrating light brown, cream, and a touch of light yellow, I've infused a warm and welcoming essence that feels just like home. The layout was structured to grab the attention of the users from their very first glance. With clean lines and organized sections, I've made sure that every browsing experience is user-friendly.
